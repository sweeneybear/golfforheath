An annual charity golf tournament in honor of Heath Lee Pierson

Charity Golf Tournament

Saturday
July 14, 2018
1 p.m. at Willis Case Golf Course

Each year around Heath's Birthday (July 10), we gather to to celebrate his life with a golf tournament and reception. 100% of funds raised through golf, reception and raffle tickets are contributed to the Heath Pierson Memorial Fund, a college savings account for Heath's neice and god daughter.

We look forward to seeing you this summer!

Tickets and Registration

Golf and Reception - $100

Includes green and cart fees, gift bag, food and beverage at the reception and five raffle tickets.

Reception only - $30

Includes food and beverage at the reception.

Contact Us

This tournament is organized by a group of Heath's friends. If you have any questions, please give us a shout!
